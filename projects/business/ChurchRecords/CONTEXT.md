# ChurchRecords

## What This Is
Validating demand for a self-serve church record-keeping SaaS. Narrow scope: membership, attendance, sacramental records (baptisms, marriages, funerals), and visitor tracking. No giving/communication tools.

## Status
**Active — validation phase.** Survey sent to 34+ churches. Waiting on responses.

## The Problem
Churches manage critical records (membership, baptisms, marriages, funerals, visitor follow-up) on paper or spreadsheets. Existing ChMS tools (Planning Center, Breeze, Realm) are bloated and expensive. Small churches under 150 members have no affordable, simple option.

## Competition
- **Breeze** — closest competitor, ~$72/mo, started simple but got bloated. Former Breeze users = early adopters.
- **The Church Continuity Platform™** (Jessica Shunta) — done-for-you consulting service, $997 setup + $297/mo. Proof the market exists. Our angle: self-serve, cheaper, church owns their data.
- **Planning Center, Realm, CCB** — too big, too expensive for small churches.

## Our Angle
Self-serve software. Church runs it themselves. $20–$49/mo target. No setup fee. No consultant dependency.
