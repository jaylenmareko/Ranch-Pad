**Why this type:**
- Most cross-platform creator type on LinkedIn (also heavy on TikTok/FinTok, Instagram, YouTube)
- Audience is financially literate and willing to pay for direct creator access
- Not dependent on brand deals — actively looking for fan monetization models
- High engagement, loyal audiences

**Search Criteria:**
- Keywords: "personal finance creator", "financial educator", "money coach", "finance content creator", "FinTok", "investing educator"
- Platform signals in bio: Instagram / TikTok / YouTube links
- Follower range: 5K–500K (mid-tier — most motivated to diversify income)
- Location: US-based preferred
